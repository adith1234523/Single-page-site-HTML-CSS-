About us

The term "Vikings" historically refers to Norse seafarers and warriors
who lived during the Viking Age, roughly spanning from the late 8th century
to the early 11th century. They originated from the Scandinavian regions of
present-day Denmark, Norway, and Sweden. Vikings are known for their exploration, raids,
and trading across Europe, Asia, and even North America.
